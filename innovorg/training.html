We help your technical workforce level up their skills, and help partners get the most out of your technology by providing clear training pathways.

Introducing the Innovorg Training Module

The Innovorg Training Module addresses employee and partner skill gaps. It provides an all-in-one platform with access to internal training as well as a curated global content ecosystem. This Training Module is more than an online learning platform; it focuses on the specific skills needed, resulting in better learner engagement, and improved employee and partner onboarding and retention.

Providing Efficient Training Pathways

The Innovorg Training Module provides the resources and pathway to efficiency train individuals where they’re at. No more wasting time with material they already know. Innovorg has identified thousands of specific skills and pathways to learn them. Integration with external and internal learning tools such as LMS systems, xAPI, and Learning Record Stores allow for progress tracking.

New Business Opportunities Through Training

Do you consider the people on your team and using your technology your greatest asset? You shoud. Their skills and training help provide best-in-class applications. Don’t miss out on new business opportunities if your people don’t have the right skills and certifications. With the Innovorg Training Module, you improve employee and partner onboarding time, helping you provide amazing experiences with your technology and less frustration from employees who now have all the resources they need to.
