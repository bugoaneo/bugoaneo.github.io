Who we are

How do you effectively and continuously distribute your Product and Services knowledge internally and externally? How do you ensure everyone understands what to focus on at any given time and how learning is connected to the business outcomes they deliver? Those are the questions the founder Elya McCleave worked on when establishing Innovorg in 2018.

Innovorg is a unique skills-building and talent analytics solution built for the Cloud industry. It works with unlimited organizational and role structures, complex product and services catalogs, and any resource intensity.

Our Mission

We want to help organizations unlock full potential of their workforce and partners, while helping individuals to enter and build successful, long-term careers in the Cloud sector.

Our Vision

We want to help organizations unlock the full potential of their workforce and partners while helping individuals to enter and build successful, long-term careers in the Cloud sector.

Our Story

2019

Innovorg establishement

Vision and market fit

After almost twenty years in the Cloud industry, Elya left a very successful corporate career to build Innovorg. The vision of such a platform has been with her for many years, but she thought someone else would no doubt create the product. Initially, Elya attracted many Cloud companies as a consultant to identify all the painpoints and validate the market fit further across the entire sector.

2020

Skills Module Beta and Launch

First Paying clients signed

The company was bootstrapped to Skills Module commercialization in 2020. The first customer signed was a Cloud SaaS company, with several Managed Service Providers to follow. Great talent was attracted to join Innovorg’s mission.

2021

Pre-seed funding round closed

Team secured full-time

In late 2021 to accelerate the growth, Innovorg closed the angel round with support from the Cloud industry’s well-known CEOs as investors.

The team was secured full time and the focus was entirely on building the quality product while keeping our existing customers happy.

2022

Learn Module Launch

Certification Module Launch

Development team expansion

It has been an exciting year as we brought the new modernized Skills Module v2 to the market. We have also launched the Learning Module and are scheduled to onboard our first client to the Certification Module in October 2022. The team is growing, and we are creating a buzz in the Cloud industry.

Future

We will continue to expand our footprint in North America and internationally, becoming the go-to skills-building & talent analytics platform for the Cloud Industry. Our product roadmap has several additional modules we will bring to the market in the upcoming months.
