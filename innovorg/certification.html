Certification module

We help your technical workforce level up their skills, and help partners get the most out of your technology by providing clear training pathways.

Introducing the Certification Module

The Innovorg Certification Module keeps track of your organization’s current certifications and progress towards new certifications. This helps ensure individuals and the organization have the certifications customers demand and industries require. In conjunction with the Innovorg Skills and Training Modules, team members will know which skills set them up for certifications, and what training pathways lead them towards certifications. Once they meet the requirements, they may get industry-recognized certificates as incentives to build their careers. You can also create your own certification programs and issue digital certificates to team members and partners.

Track Certifications & Progress

The Certification Module ensures your organization and team members meet the requirements laid out by certification bodies. Find out if individuals need to update their certifications long before they expire, and identify who in your organization has the best path towards a useful certification.

Implementing your own certification program for Channel Partners is a great way to ensure your partners meet your requirements. The Certification Module takes the complexity out of managing this type of program.

Use Certifications as an Advantage

Companies able to prove their mastery of a certain subject area establishes trust, and certain industries require certifications to even bid on RFQs. Maintaining certifications unlocks these business opportunities. For technology providers, they can use the Certification Module to create their own customer and partner certification processes to increase customer satisfaction and loyalty.

Key Benefits

Meet and Maintain Compliance Requirements

Managers don’t have time to deal with team certifications. Get a clear view of your compliance both at an organization and team member level, and know when certifications need to be renewed. Integration with the Innovorg Skills Module helps ensure you have a pipeline of team members to help gain and maintain certifications.

Build your own Certification Programs

The Certification Module allows you to build your own certification pathways with either your own custom learning content or content provided by leading platforms. Track the progress of those in certification pathways, and provide coaching and additional resources where needed. This ensures team members have the specific skills they need, and a meaningful certificate of achievement.

Provide Incentives for Individuals to Learn

Certificates can be can excellent motivation for individuals to improve their skills, and can be helpful in evaluating employee performance.

Deliver Individualized Certification Path

Have visibility into team member progress to see how their skills and training fits into certification. See whether a team member is way towards a certification, and provide them a clear pathway and set of training resources to get them the whole way there.

Find and Adopt new Certifications

The platform includes more than 680+ certification providers, each with one or more certification exams.
